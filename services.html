Our Services

Our State-of-the-art banking services leverage best technology to offer seamless and secure financial solutions. These include online and mobile banking, real-time transactions, biometric authentication, and personalized financial insights. Such advancements enhance user experience, streamline operations, and ensure efficient, modern banking tailored to the evolving needs of our members.a

Mobile Banking App

A mobile banking app is a simple, convenient way to take charge of your bank account(s) and transact on your mobile phone anytime, anywhere. Regardless of your hectic schedules, with a mobile app, you can stay on top of your finances by placing requests 24*7 on the go and executing transactions in real-time.

NFET/RTGS/IMPS

Experience seamless transactions with our NFET, RTGS, and IMPS services. Swiftly transfer funds, pay bills, and manage your finances securely. Elevate your banking convenience with our advanced electronic payment solutions.

e NACH

With E-NACH payment service, you can now easily pay your loan EMIs from your bank account, without the need for paper based mandates. E-NACH stands for Electronic National Automated Clearing House. Facility for registration and / or validation of e-NACH mandate for payment of premium can be completed with Net banking or Debit card credentials. All premiums due till date are paid. Time saving process.

SIP (Standing Instruction Process)

The Standing Instructions feature facilitates periodic scheduled payments for funds transfer, third-party payment, and RTGS/NEFT/State Bank Group transactions. You can register standing instructions on your savings and current accounts. You can register multiple standing instructions on different accounts and at different frequencies.

E-Form

Clients can register online e-form for account opening and start all services related to bank service. Eforms are widely used as part of digital transformation. eforms are fast and safe for both you and the customer. After creating your account at the eKYC portal of any KRA, you need to fill in your personal details like your Aadhaar card number along with your registered phone number on which you will receive an OTP.

Core Banking Service

Mahaganpati Multistate's Core banking services encompass essential financial functions such as deposits, withdrawals, loans, and transfers, managed centrally through a core banking system. Our integrated platform ensures real-time transaction processing, account management, and seamless communication between branches, providing customers with consistent and efficient banking experiences.
